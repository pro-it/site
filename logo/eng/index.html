Logo of Trade–Union of Employees in the Field of Information Technologies
(Logo of PEFIT)








  • The Pixel Art style is used.
  • Each individual pixel symbolically displays a member of the trade union.
  • The pixel structure appeals to the building architecture, denoting the strength of the structure.
  • Combination of pixels is associated with national Belarusian ornaments.
  • The image is stylistically inscribed with the letters "i" and "т", taken from the Belarusian language, which has the official language status in the Republic of Belarus.
  • Combination of letters forms the "roof" symbolically carried through by the members of the trade union.
  • Also a white cross is inscribed in the logo as a symbol of help and support.
  • This pixel combination can be associated with QR Code.
  • The logo is square shaped.
